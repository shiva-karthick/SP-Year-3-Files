Henry Wadsworth Longfellow - a video of his home

Henry W. Longfellow (1807 - 1882), was one of the world's foremost 19th century poets. The house in which he lived in Cambridge, Massachusetts, also served as the headquarters for General George Washington during the Siege of Boston, July 1775 - April 1776.
